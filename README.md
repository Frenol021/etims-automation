# KRA eTIMS Integration Services

## 🏢 About Us
We provide professional system integration services connecting business applications to the Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) eTIMS platform.

Our goal is to help businesses achieve tax compliance through automation by linking their POS, ERP, Accounting, and Custom Systems directly to eTIMS.

We eliminate manual invoice entry and reduce compliance errors by enabling real-time electronic invoicing.

---

## 💼 Our Services

### 1. eTIMS Integration
We connect your system directly to KRA eTIMS so invoices are transmitted automatically.

Supported systems:
- Point of Sale (POS)
- ERP Systems
- Accounting Software
- SACCO Systems
- Custom Business Applications

---

### 2. API Development
We build secure APIs that allow your software to communicate with government platforms and third-party services.
